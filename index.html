隐私政策

最后更新：2025 年 4 月

感谢您使用 Zest Browser（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私，本政策说明我们如何处理您的信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

本应用不收集、不上传任何个人信息。所有数据均存储在您的设备本地，包括：

  • 浏览历史记录
  • 书签
  • 快速访问站点
  • 应用设置与偏好

2. 设备权限

本应用可能请求以下权限，仅在您访问的网页需要时使用，不用于其他目的：

  • 相机：网页拍照或视频通话
  • 麦克风：网页录音或视频通话
  • 相册：网页上传或保存图片
  • 位置：显示本地天气信息
  • 局域网：将视频投放到电视等设备

3. 广告拦截

本应用内置广告拦截功能，拦截规则在本地执行，不会将您的浏览数据发送至任何服务器。

4. 隐私模式

开启隐私模式后，本应用不会保存任何浏览历史、Cookie 或缓存数据。

5. 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方广告 SDK 或数据分析服务。您访问的网页内容由第三方网站提供，其隐私政策与本应用无关。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 政策变更

如本政策发生变更，我们将在本页面更新内容并修改"最后更新"日期。

8. 联系我们

如有任何疑问，欢迎在 App Store 留下评论，或点击开发者名称查看联系方式。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Thank you for using Zest Browser. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Zest Browser does not collect or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Browsing history
  • Bookmarks
  • Quick access sites
  • App settings and preferences

2. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions, used only when required by websites you visit:

  • Camera: For web-based photo capture or video calls
  • Microphone: For web-based audio recording or video calls
  • Photo Library: For uploading or saving images via websites
  • Location: To display local weather information
  • Local Network: To cast video to TVs or other devices

3. Ad Blocking

The built-in ad blocker runs entirely on-device. Your browsing data is never sent to any server for filtering purposes.

4. Private Mode

When Private Mode is enabled, the app does not save any browsing history, cookies, or cached data.

5. Third-Party Services

Zest Browser does not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs or analytics services. Websites you visit are governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to leave a review on the App Store, or tap the developer name to find our contact information.
